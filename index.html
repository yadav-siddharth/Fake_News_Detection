Fake News

"Falsehoods that Fool"
or "Deception in Disguise"

Fake news refers to false or misleading information presented as if it were true and intended to deceive people. This information can be spread through traditional media, social media, or other channels. Fake news can be created for a variety of reasons, including to influence public opinion, generate clicks or revenue, or to cause confusion and chaos.
